How to use SoundLink+

FOR WINDOWS ONLY


Download the Satellaview+ client v4 and run the installer.
Afterwards open the "Satellaview+" application and then
engage SoundLink+ by clicking on the button in the bottom-right
corner. If SoundLink+ is streaming, you should start hearing
either music or voice commentary!

You may also check the Soundlink+ Schedule from either
the website or within the client itself to confirm broadcast times.
